Did I Mention I Like Cars ?
❮ ❯

Growing up, I've always been a technical person, fascinated with technology. From a young age, I would spend hours watching videos about CPUs, GPUs, and various kinds of software. This curiosity and passion for understanding how things work drove me to delve deeper into the world of technology. My interest wasn't just limited to computers; it extended to all sorts of gadgets and electronic devices. This early engagement with technology laid a strong foundation for my skills in programming and coding, which began as a hobby but soon became a significant part of my life.

Alongside my love for technology, I have an equally strong passion for automobiles. The intricate engineering and design of cars captivate me, and I dream of one day merging these two interests into a successful business. My goal is to create innovative solutions that combine the latest in technology with the automotive industry, driving forward advancements and making a tangible impact. Ultimately, if someone were to ask me, "Hey Hassan, what's the end goal for you in life?" I would respond, "To be the best version of myself in every aspect of life, not just for myself but also for my friends and family of today and tomorrow."
